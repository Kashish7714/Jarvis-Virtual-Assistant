# Code of Conduct

## Summary

This project follows a simple rule: be respectful. Everyone interacting with this repository — through issues, pull requests, or discussions — is expected to treat others professionally and constructively.

---

## Expected behaviour

- Communicate clearly and respectfully
- Accept constructive feedback without taking it personally
- Focus on the technical problem, not the person
- Give credit where it is due

---

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Dismissing or belittling contributions based on someone's background or experience level

---

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported by contacting the maintainer directly via the email on their GitHub profile. All reports will be handled seriously and confidentially. The maintainer reserves the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban users who do not follow this code of conduct.
